How to make a delicous lasagna!

Hopefully your's looks like this...

This lasagna recipe will teach you how to make a quick and easy but declisious meal of lasagna for your friends and family...and possibly cat???

The list of ingredients is as follows:

  • Lasagna sheet pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Parmesian Cheese

This is a fairly simple recipe, the steps are as follows:

  1. Partially boil the Sheet pasta until it is no longer hard.
  2. Strain it and lay it in a deep ceramic pan for baking
  3. Add a layer of sheet pasta on the bottom, then add the beef and tomato sauce
  4. Layer more pasta and more beef and tomato sauce with ricotta cheese this time
  5. On top of the final layer of pasta shred the parmesian cheese
  6. Bake at 400 for 1 hr
